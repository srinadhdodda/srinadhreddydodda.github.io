Project Overview

Conducted an in-depth analysis of a dataset focused on the persistent demand for classic office supplies such as pens and notebooks in the digital age. The analysis aimed to evaluate three distinct sales strategies to determine the most effective approach for maximizing revenue and optimizing resource allocation. Through this project, we seek to provide data-driven insights to enhance sales efforts and improve overall customer satisfaction.

Data Analysis Workflow

  • Data Structuring and Cleansing: Ensuring the accuracy and reliability of the raw data by addressing inconsistencies and errors.
  • In-Depth Data Analysis: Identifying key trends and patterns to inform strategic decisions.
  • Data Visualization: Developing clear and compelling visual representations to effectively communicate analytical results.

Tools Used

  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
